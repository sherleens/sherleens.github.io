Brief Bio

I am a second-year M.S. student of CV Lab in College of Computer and Control Engineering at Nankai University (NKU). I am supervised by Prof. Jufeng Yang. My research interests are primarily on computer vision and deep learning, especially visual sentiment analysis. During my research, I am very appreciate to work with Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng (Nankai University), Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang (UC Merced), Prof. Paul L. Rosin and Prof. Yu-Kun Lai (Cardiff University).
